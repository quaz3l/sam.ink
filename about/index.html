Hi I’m Sam.

Sam I am.

I don’t like spam.

I try to write,
regarding the things,
learned,
seen,
heard,
along the way.

If I don’t,
I usually tweet,
babbling on,
about things near.

In time I will get a job,
however today,
I want to play,
think,
sing,
run,
fly.
I want to see what I can accomplish.

Throw off the bowlines,
set sail,
for a world unknown.

Sam Schooler
